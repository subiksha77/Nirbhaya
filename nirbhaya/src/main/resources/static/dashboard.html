Safety Tips

Smart, simple steps can make a big difference. Be aware, be prepared, be safe.

Your Safety is Our Priority

Empowering you with knowledge today for a safer tomorrow.

1. Be Aware of Surroundings

Stay alert to your surroundings and avoid distractions while traveling.

2. Share Your Location

Share your live location with trusted family or friends while traveling.

3. Travel in Well-Lit Areas

Prefer well-lit and crowded places. Avoid isolated or dark areas.

4. Keep Emergency Numbers Handy

Save important numbers like police (112), women helpline (1091) and family.

5. Trust Your Instincts

If something feels wrong, trust your instincts and remove yourself from the situation.

6. Carry a Safety Device

Carry a whistle, pepper spray or any safety device for emergencies.

7. Inform Your Close Ones

Let your family or friends know about your travel plans and updates.

8. Learn Self Defense

Learn basic self-defense techniques. Confidence is your best protection.

Emergency Numbers

Police 112
Women Helpline 1091

Safe Travel Reminders

  • Verify cab/auto details before starting your journey.
  • Avoid sharing personal information with strangers.
  • Keep your phone charged and internet active.
  • Use Nirbhaya SOS feature in case of danger.

ABOUT NIRBHAYA —

The Story Behind NIRBHAYA

"Nirbhaya" is a name that represents courage, strength, and the spirit of fighting against injustice. It was inspired by a tragic incident that shook the entire nation of India on 16th December 2012. A young woman fought bravely for her life, but the incident highlighted the urgent need for women's safety, security, and respect in our society.

NIRBHAYA – Women Safety System is our humble effort towards building a safer world where every woman can live freely, fearlessly, and with dignity.

She was not just a name in the news, She became a voice of millions."

— NIRBHAYA
